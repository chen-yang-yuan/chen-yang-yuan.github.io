About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Biostatistics at Emory University jointly advised by Dr. Jian Hu and Dr. Zhaohui "Steve" Qin. My research interests center around statistical genomics and computational biology, with a particular focus on machine learning and its applications in spatial and single-cell genomics data. Before joining Emory, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, where I initiated my research under the guidance of Dr. Tianwei Yu and Dr. Jicong Fan.
