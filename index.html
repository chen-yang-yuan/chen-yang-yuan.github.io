About Me

Hi, I am Chenyang Yuan, a first year PhD student in Biostatistics at Emory University. Prior to joining Emory, I received my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen) in 2022.

I am interested in research topics related to statistical genetics and genomics. I am currently working with Dr. Jian Hu on spatial transcriptomics data. I also have some experience in single-cell clustering under the guidance of Prof. Tianwei Yu and Prof. Jicong Fan at CUHK-Shenzhen.

I am also involved in collaborative work. I am currently serving as a research assistant at Dr. Yong Teng's lab at Winship Cancer Institute of Emory University. I am meanwhile working with Dr. Yi-An Ko on cardiovascular diseases.
